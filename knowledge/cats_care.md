# Cat Daily Care Routine

Adult cats typically need 15 to 30 minutes of active play per day, broken into 2 or 3 short sessions. Indoor cats especially benefit from interactive play to prevent obesity and behavior issues.

**Feeding**: most adult cats do well on 2 meals per day (morning and evening). Kittens under 6 months need 3 to 4 meals per day. Always provide fresh water in a clean bowl, ideally placed away from the food.

**Litter box**: scoop at least once daily. The general rule is one box per cat plus one extra. Cats are very sensitive to dirty boxes and may stop using them.

**Grooming**: short-haired cats benefit from weekly brushing; long-haired breeds (Persian, Maine Coon, Ragdoll) need brushing 3 to 5 times per week to prevent mats.

Foods toxic to cats: onions, garlic, chocolate, grapes, raisins, raw fish (long-term), lilies (highly toxic — even pollen).
